# Plans And Limits

## MVP Plans

| Plan | Sites | Uptime Interval | Users | History | Projects | Webhooks |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Free | 1 | 15 min | 1 | 7 days | No | No |
| Basic | 5 | 5 min | 3 | 90 days | No | Yes |
| Agency | 25+ | 1-3 min | Multiple | 180+ days | Yes | Yes |
| Enterprise | Contract | Contract | Contract | Contract | Yes | Yes |

## MVP Billing Scope

MVP implements plan model and limit checks, but does not require recurring payments.

## Enforced Limits

- site count;
- uptime interval;
- user count;
- history retention;
- projects/clients availability;
- webhook availability.

## Post-MVP

- recurring billing provider;
- invoices;
- trial flow;
- plan upgrades/downgrades;
- payment failure handling.
